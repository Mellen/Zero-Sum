Zero Sum Help

Objective

The aim of Zero Sum is to create rows or columns of numbers that add up to 0.

Gameplay

Control

The game is played with the left and right cursor keys and the space bar.

  • The cursor keys move the falling number left and right
  • The sace bar makes the falling number drop as far as it can almost immediately

You can pause and unpause the game at any point, with the pause and unpause buttons. You can also restart the game with the restart button.

Scoring

When a continuous vertical or horizontal set of two or more numbers adds up to zero then those numbers will disappear and your score will increase.

The score is calculated according ot how many numbers are eliminated in one go. 2 numbers gets you 1 point, 3 get you 3 points, 4 gets you 6, 5 gets you 10 points, and so on.

Difficulty

The zeroth level of the game is very easy to play. In fact you don't even have to touch the keyboard to get through it.

After the zeroth level the numbers start falling a little faster and there is a chance that a block will fall. Blocks cannot be used to form zero sums, but they do dissappear after an amount of time.

The higher your level, the faster the numbers fall and the longer the blocks stay for.

You gain a level for every 70 numbers cleared.
